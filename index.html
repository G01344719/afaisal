Ethical Guidelines for Developers

As software developers, it's important to follow ethical guidelines that prioritize the well-being of users. This includes ensuring privacy, security, and fairness in the products we create. Below are some key ethical considerations:

  • Protecting user data and privacy.
  • Ensuring transparency in algorithms.
  • Minimizing harm to society through technology.
